Hi, Welcome to my web page! I am originally from Mumbai, India.

Mumbai, located on India's West Coast, is the capital city of State of Maharashtra and financial capital of India. It is the largest and one of the most populated cities in India. Mumbai is a major trading center since many decades and majority of people have migrated to this city since the time it was ruled by British. Due to these factors, Mumbai has blend of many cultures, religions, festivals and cuisines. As densely populated as it is, Mumbai is known as ‘City that never sleeps’ and its population represents true spirit of India due to its diversity. Mumbai is certainly one of the greatest places to visit if one believes in living life to the fullest!
